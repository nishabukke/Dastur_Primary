Principal's Message

A Word from Our Principal

Dear All,
Teaching and learning offer us numerous opportunities to focus on a number of small things that will culminate over time to produce individuals who are capable to achieve great things in life.


Having started its journey way back in the year 1953 exclusively for the Zoroastrian minority community, today Bai Najamai Noshirwan Dastur School caters to all categories of students in our society. We, as Dasturians, have always and will continue to stand proudly for our school motto - “Good Thoughts, Good Words and Good Deeds”. Value based education to groom the youngsters to responsible citizens through a holistic approach is our asset.


Aristotle said “Educating the mind without educating the heart is no education at all”. Bai Najamai Noshirwan Dastur School’s commitment to the educational fraternity provides them with all the necessary elements for a successful future.


I strongly believe in teamwork. My sincere advice to all our parents is to spend quality time with your children. Your role in moulding them to become the finest human beings is always important. We ensure our companionship throughout this journey. A conducive environment for the kids and the school as a whole is assured by our management and each and every staff.

Primarily we as individuals can only rise above our circumstances and achieve success if we are dedicated and passionate about what we do. Therefore, as we look ahead into the 21st century, I would simply put “To Motivate the Weak, to Address the Average and To Challenge the Gifted” as the Vision of our school.


With divine assistance let us work together as a family to build on the traditions of the Past, educate the children of the Present and make them worthy for the challenges of the future. Let this be our mission.

Mrs. Leena D. Weilson

Bai Najamai Noshirwan Dastur Primary School is a recognized co-educational Primary School founded on 21st September 1953.
