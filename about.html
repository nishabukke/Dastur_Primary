School History


The foundation of Primary Education by Sardar Dastur Schools Trust goes back to the establishment of Primary Wing in Sardar Dastur Noshirwan Girls High School which was established in 1893. Later, to commemorate the Golden Jubilee of the Girls' School, a Nursery Department was started in 1953.


Till the establishment of Sardar Dastur Hoshang Boys' High School in 1912, the Primary Wing was also open to Parsee boys up to 9 years. During the period March to June 1953, a Primary and Preparatory School for boys was started in Sardar Dastur Hoshang Boys High School.


In June 1970, the Boys and Girls Primary / Nursery School was officially amalgamated. The new building to house the Nursery sections was completed in 2006.

Our Motto


The guiding principle of our school is embodied in the words “Humata”, “Hukata”, “Huvarashta” (Good Thoughts, Good Words and Good Deeds).

WE ARE DASTURIANS


We are Dasturians, so bold and brave,
We're happy children, honest and true,
Love and gratitude, we offer you,
Dear Alma Mater, we're proud of you.


Our life's first lessons, we learnt them here,
Respect our elders, to be sincere,
Obey our parents and teachers dear,
We learn all this in our Life's Best Years.


Good thoughts, Good words, Good deeds,
is our motto to succeed,
Good thoughts, Good words, Good deeds,
is our prayer, God give us the lead.


We're simple, noble Dasturians,
We try to do the right,
God is always on our side,
We keep walking in His Light.


Miss S. S. Doctor

Our Houses

To inculcate competitive spirit and espirit de corps, the girls are grouped into 4 houses –

  • Tata (Green House)
  • Bhabha (Violet House)
  • Dastur (Yellow House)
  • Wadia (Red House)
