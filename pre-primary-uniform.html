Uniform

  • Home
  • School Uniform
The School endeavours to inculcate habits of cleanliness and hygiene. Parents are therefore, requested to see that their children are always smartly dressed, not only to school but also, outside school and at public functions.

General Instructions

  • Uniform should be tailor made to fit the child decently as per his / her size.
  • School uniform socks should be ankle length and white in colour with blue and red stripes. There should be no logo / motif / emblem on it.
  • Students to wear ONLY black Sparx shoe with Velcro straps.
  • Nails should be clipped short and kept clean at all times.
  • School uniforms should be clean, neat and properly ironed each day.

Boys Uniform

  • T-Shirt : Blue T-shirt with round neck.
  • Shorts : Maroon check shorts with elastic belt and one pocket.

Girls Uniform

  • T-Shirt : Blue T-shirt with round neck.
  • Skirt : Maroon check skirt with elastic belt. Girls must wear blue bloomers under the skirt.
  • Hairs : Girls should have their hair neatly tied in two plaits if it is very long with a middle parting. Each plait should be tied with a black satin ribbon. A black hair band should be used to keep all the hair neat and from falling on to the face. Two ponies for short hair. Black hair band for very short hair.
